# Onboarding

1. Follow-up for first 5 weeks: Compliance training (1st week), Technical Onboarding (document), enrolled AE and any other onboarding (2nd and 3rd week) - took around 3 weeks, maybe less.
2. Let buddy ask freely instead of presenting things
3. 1:1 weekly (30 min, with free time after) and 2nd checkpoint (~15 min): org structure, squad composition, expectations
4. Add to required channels
